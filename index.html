Biography

    I am a third-year Ph.D. student at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) supervised by Prof. Zhiguo Cao (expected to graduate in June 2026). My research interests include 3D computer vision, video temporal consistency, dense prediction, and autonomous driving. From Nov. 2023 to Nov. 2024, I work as a visiting student at Prof. Guosheng Lin's group in S-Lab@NTU, Singapore. From APR. 2022 to Aug. 2024, I cooperate with Dr. Jianming Zhang in Adobe Research.
